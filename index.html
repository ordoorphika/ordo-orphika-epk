Bio

Ordo Orphika is an occult-inspired rock band based in Seattle, weaving stories of shadow, ritual, and transformation with thunderous riffs and haunting melodies. Fueled by arcane mythologies and electric energy, their sound blends the heavy, the surreal, and the sublime.
